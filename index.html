Welcome Home

Living Room

This Virtual Reality home is my vision of the contemporary home in the future. This is a modern and spacious room for everyone, including family, friends, and guests. The comfortable leather sofas invite everyone to relax, chat, and watch movies or TV. The painting and carpet tie together the space. The grand piano is one of my favorite components of the room, as music reverberates throughout the home.

Kitchen and Dining Room

The beautiful floor windows in the dining room and kitchen elevate the experience of cooking and enjoying food with company. The stools around the island welcome guests to sip on drinks. The dining area features the beautiful view of the outside scenery, perfecting the uplifting ambience of the home.

Study Room

This study room is designed to surround you with inspiration, with the comfortable seats, center table, and open bookshelf.

Bedroom

Tick tock. Tick tock. Here is where you get your hours of beauty sleep.

Pool Space

This is the perfect space for you to challenge your friends to a friendly game of pool. The abstract paintings serve as dramatic backdrop for the space.

Entertainment Room

This room is ideal for both work and play. It features a desk and computer area as well as a luxurious sofa for reading and watching shows.
